There are two pages in the setup manual, each with a big black number on the left. Thus, there are two total steps.

### Step 1:
- **Parts Needed:** Seat Panel (1), Side Frame (2), Support Bar (0)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Side Frame:** Connect the side frame (2) to one end of the seat panel (1).
  - **Install Support Bar:** Secure the support bar (0) horizontally between the side frame (2) and the seat panel (1) for stability.

### Step 2:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 1, Side Frame (3)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Second Side Frame:** Connect the second side frame (3) to the opposite end of the seat panel (1), completing the bench structure.
