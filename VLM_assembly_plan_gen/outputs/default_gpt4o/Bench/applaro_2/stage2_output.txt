There are three pages of the setup manual, each with a big black number on the left. Thus, there are three total steps.

### Step 1:
- **Parts Needed:** Backrest Panel (3), Side Panel (0), Side Panel (2)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Assemble Frame:** Connect the backrest panel (3) between the two side panels (0) and (2) to form the initial frame structure.

### Step 2:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 1
- **Instructions:**
  - **Stabilize Structure:** Ensure the frame from Step 1 is securely positioned and stabilized as shown in the manual.

### Step 3:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 2, Seat Panel (1), Armrest (4), Armrest (5)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Seat and Armrests:** Secure the seat panel (1) to the frame. Then, attach the armrests (4) and (5) on either side of the structure to complete the assembly.
