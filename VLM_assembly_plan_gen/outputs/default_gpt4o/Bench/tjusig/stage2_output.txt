There are six pages in the setup manual, each with a big black number on the left. Thus, there are six total steps.

### Step 1:
- **Parts Needed:** Support Frame (4), Support Frame (5)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Assemble Support Frames:** Connect the two support frames (4) and (5) together as shown in the manual.

### Step 2:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 1, Side Panel (1), Side Panel (2)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Side Panels:** Secure the side panels (1) and (2) to the subassembly from Step 1.

### Step 3:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 2, Back Panel (8), Back Panel (9)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Install Back Panels:** Attach back panels (8) and (9) to the existing structure.

### Step 4:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 3, Back Panel (10), Back Panel (11), Back Panel (12), Back Panel (13)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Complete Back Structure:** Add back panels (10), (11), (12), and (13) to complete the back enclosure.

### Step 5:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 4, Top Panel (0)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Top Panel:** Secure the top panel (0) to the top of the structure.

### Step 6:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 5, Bottom Panel (3), Shelf (6), Shelf (7)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Install Shelves and Bottom Panel:** Attach shelves (6) and (7) and the bottom panel (3) to the structure to complete the assembly.
