There are six pages in the setup manual, each with a big black number on the left. Thus, there are six total steps.

### Step 1:
- **Parts Needed:** Side Panel (0), Side Panel (1), Side Panel (2), Side Panel (3), Shelf (4)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Assemble Base:** Attach the side panels (0, 1, 2, 3) vertically to the shelf (4) to form the base structure.

### Step 2:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 1, Shelf (5)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Add Second Shelf:** Place the second shelf (5) on top of the side panels from the subassembly in Step 1.

### Step 3:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 2, Side Panel (0), Side Panel (1), Side Panel (2), Side Panel (3)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Extend Structure:** Attach additional side panels (0, 1, 2, 3) vertically on top of the existing structure.

### Step 4:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 3, Shelf (6)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Add Third Shelf:** Place the third shelf (6) on top of the newly added side panels.

### Step 5:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 4, Side Panel (0), Side Panel (1), Side Panel (2), Side Panel (3)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Further Extend Structure:** Attach more side panels (0, 1, 2, 3) vertically on top of the current structure.

### Step 6:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 5, Shelf (7)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Complete with Top Shelf:** Place the final shelf (7) on top of the structure to complete the assembly.
