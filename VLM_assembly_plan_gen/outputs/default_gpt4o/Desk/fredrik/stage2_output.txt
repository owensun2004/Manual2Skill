There are four pages in the setup manual, each with a big black number on the left. Thus, there are four total steps.

### Step 1:
- **Parts Needed:** Table Leg (0), Table Leg (1), Side Support (2)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Connect Side Support to Legs:** Attach side support (2) between table legs (0) and (1) as shown in the manual.

### Step 2:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 1, Side Support (3)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Add Second Side Support:** Attach the second side support (3) parallel to the first one, connecting the table legs (0) and (1).

### Step 3:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 2, Crossbar (4), Crossbar (5)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Install Crossbars:** Attach crossbars (4) and (5) between the side supports (2) and (3) to enhance stability.

### Step 4:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 3, Tabletop (6)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Secure Tabletop:** Place the tabletop (6) on top of the assembled frame and secure it as indicated in the manual.
