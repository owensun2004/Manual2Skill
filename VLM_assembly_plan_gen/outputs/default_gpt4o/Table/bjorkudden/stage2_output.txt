There are five pages in the setup manual, each with a big black number on the left. Thus, there are five total steps.

### Step 1:
- **Parts Needed:** Table Top (0), Support Beam (1)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Support Beam:** Secure the support beam (1) to the underside of the table top (0) as shown in the manual.

### Step 2:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 1, Leg (2), Leg (3)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Legs:** Connect the first two legs (2) and (3) to the support beam and table top assembly from Step 1.

### Step 3:
- **Parts Needed:** Side Support (6), Side Support (7)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Prepare Side Supports:** Assemble the side supports (6) and (7) together as shown in the manual.

### Step 4:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 2, Subassembly from Step 3
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Side Supports:** Connect the assembled side supports (6) and (7) to the legs and support beam structure from Step 2.

### Step 5:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 4, Leg (4), Leg (5), Side Support (8)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Complete Leg Assembly:** Attach the remaining legs (4) and (5) and the final side support (8) to complete the table structure.
