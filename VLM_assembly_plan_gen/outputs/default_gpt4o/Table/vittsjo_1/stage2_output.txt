There are three pages in the setup manual, each with a big black number on the left. Thus, there are three total steps.

### Step 1:
- **Parts Needed:** Large Round Shelf (3), Vertical Support (4), Support Bar (0)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Assemble Base Structure:** Attach the vertical supports (4) to the large round shelf (3). Connect the support bar (0) between the vertical supports to provide stability.

### Step 2:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 1, Small Round Shelf (1), Small Round Shelf (2)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Lower Shelves:** Secure the small round shelves (1) and (2) to the vertical supports, forming the lower section of the table.

### Step 3:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 2, Large Round Shelf (3)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Complete Table:** Place the large round shelf (3) on top of the structure, aligning it with the vertical supports to complete the table assembly.
