There are three pages in the setup manual, each with a big black number on the left. Thus, there are three total steps.

### Step 1:
- **Parts Needed:** Table Frame (0), Cross Support (3)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Assemble Frame and Cross Support:** Connect the cross support (3) to the table frame (0) to form the base structure.

### Step 2:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 1, Circular Support (2)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Circular Support:** Place the circular support (2) on top of the assembled frame from Step 1, ensuring it is securely connected.

### Step 3:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 2, Table Top (1)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Secure Table Top:** Position the table top (1) onto the circular support (2) and ensure it is properly aligned and attached.
