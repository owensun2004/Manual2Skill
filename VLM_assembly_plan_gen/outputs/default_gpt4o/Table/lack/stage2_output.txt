There are two pages in the setup manual, each with a big black number on the left. Thus, there are two total steps.

### Step 1:
- **Parts Needed:** Table Top (0), Leg (2), Leg (3), Leg (4), Leg (5)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Legs to Table Top:** Securely attach each of the four legs (2, 3, 4, 5) to the underside of the table top (0) as shown in the manual. Ensure each leg is aligned and tightened properly.

### Step 2:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 1, Shelf (1)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Install Shelf:** Position the shelf (1) between the legs, attaching it to the inner sides of the legs. Make sure the shelf is level and securely fastened to provide additional support and functionality.
