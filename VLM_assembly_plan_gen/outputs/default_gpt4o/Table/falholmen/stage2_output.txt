There are two pages of the setup manual, each with a big black number on the left. Thus, there are two total steps.

### Step 1:
- **Parts Needed:** Tabletop (3), Leg Frame (0), Support Bar (1)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Leg Frames:** Position the leg frames (0) vertically on the underside of the tabletop (3).
  - **Secure Support Bars:** Connect the support bars (1) horizontally between the leg frames to stabilize the structure.

### Step 2:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 1, Crossbar (2)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Install Crossbar:** Attach the crossbar (2) diagonally between the leg frames to reinforce the table's structure.
