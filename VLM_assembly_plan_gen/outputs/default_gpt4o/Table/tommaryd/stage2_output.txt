There are six pages in the setup manual, each with a big black number on the left. Thus, there are six total steps.

### Step 1:
- **Parts Needed:** Table Top (0), Side Frame (9), Side Frame (10)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Side Frames:** Secure side frames (9) and (10) to the underside of the table top (0) as shown in the manual.

### Step 2:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 1, Side Frame (11)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Add Side Frame:** Attach side frame (11) to the subassembly from Step 1, completing the frame around the table top.

### Step 3:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 2, Leg (1), Leg (2)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Install Legs:** Attach legs (1) and (2) to the corners of the subassembly from Step 2.

### Step 4:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 3, Leg (3), Leg (4)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Install Remaining Legs:** Attach legs (3) and (4) to the remaining corners of the subassembly from Step 3.

### Step 5:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 4, Support Bar (5), Support Bar (6)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Support Bars:** Connect support bars (5) and (6) horizontally between the legs on one side of the table.

### Step 6:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 5, Support Bar (7), Support Bar (8)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Remaining Support Bars:** Connect support bars (7) and (8) horizontally between the legs on the opposite side of the table, completing the structure.
