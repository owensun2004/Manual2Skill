### Step 1:
- **Parts Needed:** 
  - Side Leg (0)
  - Side Support (1)
  - Side Leg (2)
  - Support Bar (3)
  - Table Top (4)

- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Side Legs and Supports:** 
    - Connect the side legs (0 and 2) to the side support (1) to form the sides of the table.
  - **Install Support Bar:**
    - Attach the support bar (3) between the side supports to enhance stability.
  - **Position Table Top:**
    - Place the table top (4) on the assembled frame, ensuring it is securely attached to the legs and supports.
