There are two pages of the setup manual, each with a big black number on the left. Thus, there are two total steps.

### Step 1:
- **Parts Needed:** Base (6)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Wheels:** Insert the wheels into the designated slots on the base (6) as shown in the manual.

### Step 2:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 1, Seat (7)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Connect Seat to Base:** Align the seat (7) with the top of the base assembly from Step 1 and secure it in place as depicted in the manual.
