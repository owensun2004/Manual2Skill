There are five pages in the setup manual, each with a big black number on the left. Thus, there are five total steps.

### Step 1:
- **Parts Needed:** Side Frame (1), Support Bar (3)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Support Bar:** Connect the support bar (3) to the side frame (1) as shown in the manual.

### Step 2:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 1, Side Frame (2)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Add Second Side Frame:** Attach the second side frame (2) to the existing assembly from Step 1.

### Step 3:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 2, Seat (4)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Install Seat:** Place the seat (4) onto the assembled frame from Step 2.

### Step 4:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 3, Backrest (0)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Backrest:** Secure the backrest (0) to the assembly from Step 3.

### Step 5:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 4, Support Bar (5)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Add Final Support Bar:** Connect the second support bar (5) to the back of the assembly for additional stability.
