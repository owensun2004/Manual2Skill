There are four pages in the setup manual, each with a big black number on the left. Thus, there are four total steps.

### Step 1:
- **Parts Needed:** Side Leg (1), Support Bar (2), Support Bar (3)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Support Bars:** Connect the support bars (2) and (3) to the side leg (1) as shown in the manual.

### Step 2:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 1, Side Leg (4)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Join Second Side Leg:** Attach the second side leg (4) to the subassembly from Step 1, ensuring the support bars are aligned properly.

### Step 3:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 2, Backrest (5)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Backrest:** Secure the backrest (5) to the assembled frame from Step 2, aligning it with the side legs.

### Step 4:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 3, Seat (0)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Install Seat:** Place the seat (0) onto the frame, ensuring it is properly aligned and secured to the side legs and backrest.
