There are four pages in the setup manual, each with a big black number on the left. Thus, there are four total steps.

### Step 1:
- **Parts Needed:** Leg Frame (0), Leg Frame (1)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Connect Leg Frames:** Attach leg frame (0) to leg frame (1) as shown in the manual. Ensure they are aligned properly to form part of the chair's base.

### Step 2:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 1, Leg Frame (2)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Complete Base Structure:** Connect leg frame (2) to the subassembly from Step 1 to complete the chair's base.

### Step 3:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 2, Backrest (3)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Backrest:** Secure the backrest (3) to the assembled base structure from Step 2. Ensure it clicks into place for stability.

### Step 4:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 3, Seat (4)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Install Seat:** Place the seat (4) onto the assembled structure from Step 3. Press down until it clicks securely into place.
