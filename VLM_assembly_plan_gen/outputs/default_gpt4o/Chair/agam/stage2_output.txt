There are six pages in the setup manual, so there are six total steps.

### Step 1:
- **Parts Needed:** Seat (8), Backrest (9)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Backrest to Seat:** Connect the backrest (9) to the seat (8) by aligning the backrest rods with the holes in the seat.

### Step 2:
- **Parts Needed:** Front Leg (0), Back Leg (3), Side Rail (1)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Connect Side Rail to Legs:** Attach the side rail (1) between the front leg (0) and the back leg (3).

### Step 3:
- **Parts Needed:** Front Rail (4), Back Rail (5)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Rails to Legs:** Connect the front rail (4) and back rail (5) to the corresponding legs to form a rectangular base.

### Step 4:
- **Parts Needed:** Side Rail (2)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Complete Base Structure:** Attach the second side rail (2) to the opposite side of the base, connecting the front and back legs.

### Step 5:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 1, Subassembly from Step 4
- **Instructions:**
  - **Combine Seat and Base:** Secure the seat and backrest assembly from Step 1 onto the base structure from Step 4.

### Step 6:
- **Parts Needed:** Seat Support (6), Seat Support (7)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Add Seat Supports:** Attach the seat supports (6) and (7) underneath the seat to provide additional stability.
