There are three pages in the setup manual, each with a big black number on the left. Thus, there are three total steps.

### Step 1:
- **Parts Needed:** Backrest (0), Front Leg Frame (1)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Connect Frames:** Attach the backrest (0) to the front leg frame (1) as shown in the manual. Ensure the frames are aligned properly.

### Step 2:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 1, Side Support Bar (2)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Support Bar:** Connect the side support bar (2) to the subassembly from Step 1. This will add stability to the frame structure.

### Step 3:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 2, Seat (3)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Install Seat:** Secure the seat (3) onto the assembled frame from Step 2. Make sure it is firmly attached and aligned with the frame.
