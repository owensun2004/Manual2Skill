There are nine pages in the setup manual, each with a big black number on the left. Thus, there are nine total steps.

### Step 1:
- **Parts Needed:** Side Leg (1), Support Bar (5)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Support Bar:** Connect the support bar (5) to the side leg (1) as shown in the manual.

### Step 2:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 1, Side Leg (2), Support Bar (6)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Complete Leg Frame:** Attach the second side leg (2) and support bar (6) to the existing subassembly.

### Step 3:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 2, Back Leg (7)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Add Back Leg:** Connect the back leg (7) to the assembled frame.

### Step 4:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 3, Backrest Frame (4)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Backrest Frame:** Secure the backrest frame (4) to the existing structure.

### Step 5:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 4, Seat Frame (0)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Connect Seat Frame:** Attach the seat frame (0) to the assembled structure.

### Step 6:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 5, Support Bar (3)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Add Support Bar:** Connect the support bar (3) to the frame for additional stability.

### Step 7:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 6, Backrest (9)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Install Backrest:** Attach the backrest (9) to the backrest frame.

### Step 8:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 7, Support Bar (6)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Reinforce Structure:** Secure the support bar (6) to the frame for enhanced stability.

### Step 9:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 8, Seat (8)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Seat:** Place and secure the seat (8) onto the seat frame, ensuring it clicks into place.
