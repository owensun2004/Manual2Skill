There are three pages in the setup manual, each with a big black number on the left. Thus, there are three total steps.

### Step 1:
- **Parts Needed:** Side Frame (1), Side Frame (4)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Connect Side Frames:** Attach side frame (1) to side frame (4) as shown in the manual. Ensure the frames are aligned properly to form the base structure.

### Step 2:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 1, Support Bar (2), Support Bar (3)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Support Bars:** Secure support bar (2) and support bar (3) between the side frames from Step 1. This will provide stability to the structure.

### Step 3:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 2, Seat (0)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Install Seat:** Place the seat (0) on top of the assembled frame from Step 2. Ensure it is centered and securely attached to complete the stool.
