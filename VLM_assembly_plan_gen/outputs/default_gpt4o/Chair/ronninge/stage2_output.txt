There are seven pages in the setup manual, each with a big black number on the left. Thus, there are seven total steps.

### Step 1:
- **Parts Needed:** Side Frame (3), Backrest Support (0)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Backrest Support:** Connect the backrest support (0) to the side frame (3) as shown in the manual.

### Step 2:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 1, Backrest Support (1)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Add Second Backrest Support:** Attach the second backrest support (1) to the opposite side of the side frame (3).

### Step 3:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 2, Side Frame (4)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Connect Second Side Frame:** Attach the second side frame (4) to the backrest supports (0 and 1).

### Step 4:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 3, Seat Support (2)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Install Seat Support:** Connect the seat support (2) between the two side frames (3 and 4).

### Step 5:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 4, Front Support (7)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Front Support:** Secure the front support (7) between the two side frames (3 and 4) at the front.

### Step 6:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 5, Backrest (5)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Mount Backrest:** Attach the backrest (5) to the backrest supports (0 and 1).

### Step 7:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 6, Seat (6)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Install Seat:** Place the seat (6) onto the seat support (2) and secure it in place.
