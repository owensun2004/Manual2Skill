There are four pages in the setup manual, each with a big black number on the left. Thus, there are four total steps.

### Step 1:
- **Parts Needed:** Leg Panel (5), Support Bar (3)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Support Bar to Leg Panel:** Connect the support bar (3) to the leg panel (5) as shown in the manual.

### Step 2:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 1, Side Panel (1)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Insert Side Panel:** Slide the side panel (1) into the subassembly from Step 1, ensuring it fits securely.

### Step 3:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 2, Top Panel (0)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Top Panel:** Secure the top panel (0) onto the subassembly from Step 2, aligning it properly.

### Step 4:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 3, Side Panel (2), Support Bar (4), Leg Panel (6)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Complete Assembly:** Attach the second side panel (2), additional support bar (4), and the second leg panel (6) to the existing structure from Step 3, ensuring all parts are firmly connected.
