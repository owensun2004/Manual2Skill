There are four pages in the setup manual, each with a big black number on the left. Thus, there are four total steps.

### Step 1:
- **Parts Needed:** Leg (3), Leg (4)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Connect Legs:** Attach leg (3) to leg (4) to form the base structure of the chair.

### Step 2:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 1, Back Support (1)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Back Support:** Connect the back support (1) to the assembled leg structure from Step 1.

### Step 3:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 2, Backrest (0)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Install Backrest:** Secure the backrest (0) to the back support (1) on the existing assembly.

### Step 4:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 3, Seat (2)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Seat:** Fix the seat (2) onto the completed frame from Step 3, ensuring it is securely in place.
