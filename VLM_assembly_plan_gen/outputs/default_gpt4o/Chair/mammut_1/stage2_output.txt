There are two pages of the setup manual, each with a big black number on the left. Thus, there are two total steps.

### Step 1:
- **Parts Needed:** Seat (0), Legs (2, 3, 4, 5)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Legs to Seat:** Connect each of the four legs (2, 3, 4, 5) to the underside of the seat (0) as shown in the manual. Ensure each leg is securely fastened.

### Step 2:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 1, Backrest (1)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Backrest to Seat:** Align the backrest (1) with the slots on the back of the seat (0) and secure it in place. Make sure the backrest is firmly attached to complete the chair assembly.
