There are three pages in the setup manual, each with a big black number on the left. Thus, there are three total steps.

### Step 1:
- **Parts Needed:** Side Leg (1), Side Leg (2), Front Leg Frame (4)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Assemble Side Legs and Front Frame:** Connect the side leg (1) and side leg (2) to the front leg frame (4) as shown in the manual page.

### Step 2:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 1, Backrest Frame (0)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Backrest Frame:** Secure the backrest frame (0) to the subassembly from Step 1, ensuring it is aligned properly.

### Step 3:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 2, Seat (3)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Install Seat:** Attach the seat (3) to the assembled frame from Step 2, making sure it is firmly connected.
