- **Instructions:**

### Step 3:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 2, Side Leg (2)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Add Second Side Leg:** Attach the second side leg (2) to the existing structure, ensuring it aligns with the corner shelf and shelf frame.

- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 3, Support Bar (4)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Install Support Bar:** Finally, connect the support bar (4) between the two side legs to reinforce the structure and provide stability.
