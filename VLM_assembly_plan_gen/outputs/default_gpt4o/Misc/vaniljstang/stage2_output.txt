There are five pages in the setup manual, each with a big black number on the left. Thus, there are five total steps.

### Step 1:
- **Parts Needed:** Side Panel (2), Bottom Frame (5)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Connect Side Panel to Bottom Frame:** Attach the side panel (2) to the bottom frame (5) as shown in the manual.

### Step 2:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 1, Bottom Frame (6)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Second Bottom Frame:** Connect the second bottom frame (6) to the existing assembly from Step 1.

### Step 3:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 2, Top Frame (0)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Add Top Frame:** Secure the top frame (0) to the structure assembled in Step 2.

### Step 4:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 3, Top Frame (1)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Second Top Frame:** Connect the second top frame (1) to the current assembly, completing the upper structure.

### Step 5:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 4, Shelf (3), Side Panel (4)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Install Shelf and Final Side Panel:** Place the shelf (3) between the side panels and attach the final side panel (4) to complete the furniture assembly.
