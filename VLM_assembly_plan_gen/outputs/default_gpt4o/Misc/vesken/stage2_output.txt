There are four pages in the setup manual, each with a big black number on the left. Thus, there are four total steps.

### Step 1:
- **Parts Needed:** Bottom Shelf (2), Legs (3, 4, 5, 6), Wheels (7, 8, 9, 10)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Wheels to Legs:** Connect each wheel (7, 8, 9, 10) to the bottom of each leg (3, 4, 5, 6).
  - **Attach Legs to Bottom Shelf:** Insert each leg into the designated slots on the bottom shelf (2).

### Step 2:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 1, Middle Shelf (1)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Position Middle Shelf:** Place the middle shelf (1) onto the legs, ensuring it is securely fitted above the bottom shelf.

### Step 3:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 2, Additional Legs (3, 4, 5, 6)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Additional Legs:** Connect the additional legs (3, 4, 5, 6) to the top of the middle shelf, aligning them with the existing structure.

### Step 4:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 3, Top Shelf (0)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Position Top Shelf:** Secure the top shelf (0) onto the newly attached legs, completing the assembly of the cart.
