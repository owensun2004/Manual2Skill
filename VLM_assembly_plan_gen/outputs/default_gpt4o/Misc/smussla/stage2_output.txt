There are six pages in the setup manual, each with a big black number on the left. Thus, there are six total steps.

### Step 1:
- **Parts Needed:** Bottom Panel (3), Back Panel (4)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Align and Attach:** Connect the back panel (4) vertically to the bottom panel (3) as shown in the manual.

### Step 2:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 1, Side Panel (1)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Side Panel:** Secure the side panel (1) to the right side of the subassembly from Step 1.

### Step 3:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 2, Back Panel (5)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Complete Back Enclosure:** Attach the back panel (5) to the left side of the subassembly to form a box shape.

### Step 4:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 3, Shelf (2)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Insert Shelf:** Place the shelf (2) inside the box, aligning it with the pre-drilled holes.

### Step 5:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 4, Top Panel (0)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Secure Top Panel:** Position the top panel (0) on top of the structure, ensuring it fits snugly.

### Step 6:
- **Parts Needed:** Subassembly from Step 5, Legs (8, 9, 10)
- **Instructions:**
  - **Attach Legs:** Secure the legs (8, 9, 10) to the bottom corners of the assembled structure for support.
